Incursion game guide

This strategy guide includes tips and tricks to defeat each battle and will walk you through the best strategies to use to master your deck as you traverse through the levels. Here, we will feature combat strategies and tips to help you conquer the land of Feldenberg.

Strategy

Incursion is a deck-building game and cards are the main game mechanic, they represent an action the champion makes. It's crucial to know how to use your deck to survive and conquer battle. There are two main types of cards: healing cards, and damage card.
Energy is required to play a card, as each card has an energy cost of 10. Energy is refilled at the end of a turn.

Cards

Cards can be played when it is that players turn and they have full energy. To play a card you must simply click on the card you want to play.
Attack Card
If you play an attack card you will damage the enemy for a random damage between 10 to 20hp.

Attack Card
You should play an attack card when your health is high and there is no risk of losing.
Heal Card
If you play a heal card you will heal both players for 10 hp.

Heal Card
It is important to remember to heal when you are at low health to avoid a loss.
